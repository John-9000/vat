Open FAQ
Is Romania VAT relevant only if we have a local company?

No. A non-established company can still face Romanian VAT obligations depending on imports, local sales, warehousing, and how the commercial chain is structured.

Can we decide VAT based only on turnover?

Not safely. For non-resident businesses, the operating model matters just as much as turnover, especially when stock, imports, or local delivery are involved.

Can you support the work after registration?

Yes. The site is structured around the full cycle: registration readiness, filings, calculator support, and evidence or process clean-up for ongoing operations.
