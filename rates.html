VAT rates

Many countries have a standard VAT rate and one or more reduced rates for specific goods/services. Because rates can change, publish them with a “last updated” note and a single source of truth in your code.

How to present rates on your site

  • Show rate categories (standard / reduced) with examples.
  • Avoid over-promising: “common applications” instead of “always.”
  • Add a last updated line and your source link(s).
  • Keep rates configurable (one file / one CMS field), not scattered across pages.
